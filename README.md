# quantum-pet-simulator

Welcome to **Quantum Pet Simulator** — the world's first (fictional) pet simulator powered by quantum weirdness!

Imagine raising digital pets that exist in superposition: your cat can be both hungry *and* fed until you observe it. Your dog might teleport across the room thanks to quantum tunneling. Pure fun, zero physics degree required.

This repo is 100% documentation: features, "design specs", fake user guides, and weekly idea logs.

## Core Concept
Pets behave according to quantum rules instead of classical ones. Observation collapses their state — just like real quantum mechanics, but way cuter.
